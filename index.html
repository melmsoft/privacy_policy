FF Guide Pro Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At FF Guide Pro, we value the privacy of our users. This privacy policy explains what data our application collects, how it is processed, and how it is protected.

2. Collected Data

FF Guide Pro may request information such as username, email address, and in-game IDs. Additionally, we may collect anonymous data for in-app analytics and error reports.

3. Use of Data

The collected data is used for the following purposes:

  • Improving user experience
  • Managing in-app rewards
  • Providing support services
  • Complying with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing

We do not share user data with third parties. However, it may be shared with authorized entities if required by law.

5. Children's Privacy

Our application does not target children under 13 years old. If you believe that we have mistakenly collected such data, you may contact us to request its deletion.

6. Security

We take necessary technical and administrative measures to protect user data. However, we cannot guarantee that data transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. User Rights

Users may request the deletion or correction of their personal data. You can contact us for such requests.
